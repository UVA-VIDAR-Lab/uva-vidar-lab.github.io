Interested in working with us?

We are always looking for talented individuals to join our team. If you are passionate about data visualization, human-centered design, and intelligent systems, we would love to hear from you.


What am I looking for in a student?

We are looking for students who are curious, creative, and eager to learn. You should have a strong interest in data visualization and design, are excited about working with and understanding people, as well as a willingness to explore new ideas and approaches. Experience with programming, design tools, or research methods is a plus, but not required.

How do I get Involved?


Current UVA Students:

If you are a current Undergraduate, Masters, or PhD currently enrolled at the University of Virginia, reach out to us via email with [current student] in the header. Include your research interests and the specific research project you are interested in working on and why you would be a good fit for the lab.



Prospective PhD Students:

If you are interested in joining our lab as a PhD student, first apply directly to the School of Data Science graduate program. Admissions are open for Fall 2026 (deadline: January 5th, 2026).

When you apply, be sure to mention my name in your personal statement, with a clear explanation of why and how I can be of help to your research and your future career. After you have applied, please send me an email with [prospective PhD student] in the header and include your CV, research interests, and any a summary of relevant publications or class projects.
