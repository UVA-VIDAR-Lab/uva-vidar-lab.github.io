Evaluation Metrics for Visualization Design


What marks a successful design? Is it the effectiveness, novelty, or affective impact of the design? How can we measure these attributes of visualizations and leverage them to support design tasks such as generating a gallery of designs or choosing the right design for a specific context? Together with collaborators, we develop corpora and metrics that characterize the structure, style, and impact of visualizations at scale.
