Intelligent Co-Design Interfaces


Automated design tools should augment rather than replace a designer's creative process. We study, build, and evaluate intelligent systems that support the design process by providing personalized design nudges, responding to fluid shifts in designer intent (e.g., between convergent and divergent thinking), and adapting strategies to support evolving creative goals.
